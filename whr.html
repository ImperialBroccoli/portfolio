Wichita Homeschool Robotics Website

Project completed November 2023





In this project, I used a 2-step process to make the Wichita Homeschool Robotics Website. From designing the layout in Canva to coding with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I'll take you through my process, sharing the challenges I faced and the valuable lessons I learned along the way.



Step 1: Designing



In the first step of designing the website, I reviewed all the media and text that needed to be displayed and began planning out the necessary pages. I created a mockup of the homepage using Canva and quickly designed the other pages based on this template. Once the layout was established, I added colors and finalized the placement of pictures and text.



Step 2: Building

I built the entire website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I started with the navbar, as it would remain the same across all pages. Then, I constructed each section of the homepage, ensuring they were mobile-friendly. This process allowed me to efficiently replicate sections from the homepage onto other pages, saving time.





Conclusion:

This project took longer than expected due to the use of raw code, but it provided valuable insights into how a website functions. Designing the layout in Canva beforehand saved a significant amount of time, as it eliminated the need to design directly in the code, which would have been much more time-consuming.
