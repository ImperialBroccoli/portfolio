Barbershop Template Website

Project completed May 2024





In this project, I used a 2-step process to make a Barbershop Template Website. From designing the layout in Figma to building it with Wordpress, I'll take you through my process, sharing the challenges I faced and the valuable lessons I learned along the way.



Step 1: Designing



In the first step of designing this website, I gathered some inspiration from Pinterest and found some royalty free images. Then I quickly designed the homepage using my new found love, Figma. Once the layout was established, I created a color palette and finalized the placement of pictures.



Step 2: Building

I built this website in Wordpress. I started with the hero section. Then, I constructed each other section of the homepage, ensuring they were mobile-friendly.





Conclusion:

The utilization of Wordpress in this project expedited the build process significantly. Pre-designing the layout in Figma proved instrumental in saving time, bypassing the necessity to design directly within Wordpress, which would have been slightly more time-consuming.
